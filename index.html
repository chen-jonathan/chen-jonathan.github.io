About Me

Hi, I'm Jonathan Chen and I am a second-year student studying Computer Science at the University of Toronto! From attending hackathons and conferences, working on side projects and taking relevant courses in software design and computer science I am actively learning new concepts, solving new problems and developing my current skillset. My interests in computer science range from machine learning and artificial intelligence, data science, and human-computer interactions.

In addition to my passion for computer science, I am also an avid basketball fan. Growing up, I would love watching NBA games and playing basketball on the streets with my friends. Basketball has opened the door for a plethora of new opportunities for me to pursue. It has allowed me to become a contributing writer for Raptors Republic and Basketball Buzz and start Daily Dribble Drive, a basketball blog I co-created. I am always exploring ways to combine my love for basketball with the skills I have developed in computer science.

Overall, as an aspiring software engineer, I am eagerly looking for opportunities that will challenge me and provide an avenue to gain valuable experience for the future.
